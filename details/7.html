Ghost of Tsushima Details - Night Street Game

Ghost of Tsushima Overview: Ghost of Tsushima is an action-adventure game set in feudal Japan during the first Mongol invasion. You play as Jin Sakai, one of the last samurai of Tsushima Island, who must defend his home and people from the Mongol threat. Story: Jin Sakai returns to his homeland after a brutal invasion by Mongols, only to find his clan decimated and his sense of honor challenged. To save Tsushima, he must embrace unconventional tactics and shed the traditional samurai code, walking the path of the “Ghost” — a stealthier, more ruthless warrior willing to do whatever it takes to push back the invaders. Gameplay Features: Open-world exploration across a wide, beautiful island; ride your horse, climb mountains, and traverse villages. Dual combat style: use honorable samurai techniques or stealth ghost tactics. Multiple samurai stances for different enemy types. A guiding wind mechanic replaces traditional waypoints. Stunning visuals, dynamic weather, and cinematic camera angles. Optional “Kurosawa Mode” for black-and-white cinematic experience. Atmosphere: Serene beauty of feudal Japan with the tension of war. Emphasizes honor, sacrifice, and survival. Genre: Action · Adventure · Open‑World Platform: PlayStation, PC Player Tips: Use stealth to thin out enemy camps before attacks. Explore widely — hidden quests, shrines, side stories. Learn different combat stances for enemies. Pay attention to the wind for points of interest.
