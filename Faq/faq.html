Frequently Asked Questions - Night Street Game

How to purchase games?

To purchase games, you must first log in to your account or create a new one if you don't have one. Then, browse the available games, add the desired game to your shopping cart, and proceed to the payment page to complete the purchase. We accept multiple payment methods such as credit cards and electronic wallets.

Is there technical support?

Yes, we provide 24/7 technical support via email or live chat on the site. If you encounter any issues, please contact us through the "Contact Us" page, and our team will assist you as soon as possible.

Are the prices fixed?

The prices displayed on the site are fixed and include taxes and shipping in most cases. However, prices may vary based on promotional offers or changes from developers. We ensure that our prices are competitive and fair.

What to do if the game doesn't work?

If the game doesn't work after purchase, please check the system requirements on the game's details page. If the issue persists, contact our customer support, and we will process a refund or replacement if necessary. We are committed to ensuring your complete satisfaction.

If you have any other questions, don't hesitate to contact us!
